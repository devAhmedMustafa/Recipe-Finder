Recipe name: Spaghetti Bolognese

Course name

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich and savory meat sauce. Perfect for a family dinner or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.
